Our Story

Our in-person tutoring services were replaced by online classes as cases of Covid-19 increased in 2020. This transformation in our teaching environment presented a number of difficulties, but it also made it possible for people from beyond the region to attend our lessons and use our resources. In 2023, we have more than 200 students learning with us, the majority of which are from Latin America, the West Indies, and the USA.

What we offer

Passports to the World is an online lecturing platform dedicated to individuals of all demographic backgrounds who would like to enhance their language vocabulary in English, Spanish, French, and Mandarin Chinese.
